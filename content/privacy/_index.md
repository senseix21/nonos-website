# Privacy

This site collects nothing.

## What we don't do

- No access logs (IP addresses not recorded)
- No cookies
- No JavaScript
- No analytics
- No tracking pixels
- No external requests
- No CDN
- No third-party fonts

## What happens when you visit

Your browser connects directly to our server. We serve static HTML and CSS. The connection is encrypted with TLS 1.3. When you leave, no record of your visit exists.
